WHY?

Tensions are rising in Catalonia. Over 90% of people voted for independence from Spain.
If Catalonia declares independence it can't use the euro anymore. The government considers creating its own currency and has received the direction of Vitalik Buterin regarding such a platform.
This is how the idea for Catalan Coin was born.

Token Features

PEER 2 PEER

P2P exchanges allow the participants of the market to trade directly with each other without any trusted third party to process all trades.

SECURE

CatalanCoin is using secure crypto algorithms to secure the transactions, to control the creation of additional units, and to verify the transfer of assets. 

LIMITED SUPPLY

There will be no more than 10,000,000 CatalanCoins. Now and forever. Thats it. For more information about AirDrop and Fair distribution policy scroll down.

LOW FEES

Grow more by paying lower fees per transaction! CatalanCoin has an average transaction fee from $0.20 to $0.50, much more cheaper than the $4.00 of Bitcoin or PayPal's 4.4% transaction fee plus a fixed fee based on currency received.

FAST

With an average block speed of less than 20 seconds the transactions have almost real-time execution. Compared to traditional banks with 1-5 Business days transaction execution time, CatalanCoin is more than 10,000 times faster!

TRANSPARENT

CatalanCoin is based on Blockchain - a shared, immutable record of transactions built from blocks and stored in a digital ledger. It relies on cryptographic techniques to allow participants in a network to interact with complete trust.
